Who we are

A woman, a cat, and a whole lot of desert to cover.

Nomadic Paws started with one question: what if Cheeto came on the hike? Turns out, cats are braver than anyone gives them credit for.

We're based in Tucson, Arizona — saguaro country, golden hour country, Catalina Mountains country. These trails are our backyard and we're learning them together, one paw at a time.
